Tavo Dienynas provides convenient access to the manodienynas.lt school diary system with built-in translation support.

FEATURES:

• Automatic Translation
Translate the entire interface to English, Russian, Polish, or Ukrainian. All translation happens on your device using Google ML Kit - no data is sent to external servers.

• Fast and Efficient
Translations are cached locally, so pages load quickly on repeat visits.

• Secure
The app only connects to manodienynas.lt. No tracking, no ads, no data collection.

• Easy to Use
Simply select your preferred language from the menu and browse the diary as usual. The app handles everything automatically.

• Works Offline
Once translation models are downloaded, translations work without internet (website access still requires connection).

SUPPORTED LANGUAGES:
• English
• Russian
• Polish
• Ukrainian

Note: This app requires login credentials from your school's manodienynas.lt system. The app does not provide or store any login information.

Perfect for students and parents who prefer to read school information in their native language.
